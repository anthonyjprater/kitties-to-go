Meet Our Cat of the Week... Khali!!

Khali's Profile

Sweet calico kitty Khali is the proud recipient of our Cat of the week award! Kahli is an extra loving, puuuurrfect little ball of extra soft fluff. Streaks of golden hair cover over the left side of her face while the right side of her face is a chalky grey, bringing two faces together with striking green eyes.

Her favorite activities include plenty of catnip mouses to toss about or cleaning and snuggling her younger sister, Pixie Puppy for afternoon naps.

Khali alerted us when their baby sister Moscato, a robo-dwarf hamster, escaped her cage in the middle of the night and was running about! Khali trapped Moscato without harm and meowed for us to return the little fluff to her cage. Without Khali we don’t know what would’ve happened to Moscato!

Extra treats for one super-kitty!
